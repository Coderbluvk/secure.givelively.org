How to Make a Donation via Bitcoin Using MoonPay

Step 1: Fill in Your Full Name

Enter your full name in the designated field. This helps us ensure accurate processing of your donation.

Step 2: Enter Your Email Address

Provide your email address where we can send you a confirmation of your donation and further updates.

Step 3: Select Amount

Choose the amount you wish to donate from the options provided. Your generosity makes a meaningful difference.

Step 4: Copy the Address to Make Donation To

Copy the Bitcoin wallet address provided below. This is where you'll send your donation.

Step 5: Click on "Make Payment"

You will be securely redirected to the MoonPay platform to complete your payment using your debit or credit card. Rest assured, your transaction is safe and encrypted.

Step 6: Complete Your Payment on MoonPay

Follow the prompts on MoonPay to finalize your donation. MoonPay accepts major debit and credit cards for your convenience.

Step 7: Upload Proof of Payment

After successfully completing your payment, upload a proof of payment for verification. This step ensures that your donation is processed promptly.

Step 8: Click on "I have made this donation"

Confirm your generous contribution by clicking this button. This helps us acknowledge your support and send you a confirmation email.

For any assistance with using MoonPay or making a Bitcoin donation, please contact MoonPay support directly at support@moonpay.com.
